## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-21

### Added

**Part I: Taxonomy of Unethical Business Practices**
- Category 1: Pricing & Monetization Manipulation (1.1–1.3)
  - Enshittification Pattern (1.1.1–1.1.5)
  - Subscription Manipulation (1.2.1–1.2.6)
  - Hidden Cost Practices (1.3.1–1.3.4)
- Category 2: Dark Patterns in User Interface Design (2.1–2.2)
  - Deceptive Design Taxonomy (2.1.1–2.1.16)
  - Emotional Manipulation Techniques (2.2.1–2.2.5)
- Category 3: Data & Privacy Violations (3.1–3.3)
  - Data Collection Abuses (3.1.1–3.1.5)
  - Consent Manipulation (3.2.1–3.2.5)
  - Data Portability Obstruction (3.3.1–3.3.4)
- Category 4: Service Quality Manipulation (4.1–4.2)
  - Artificial Degradation (4.1.1–4.1.4)
  - Support Manipulation (4.2.1–4.2.4)

### Notes

This is the initial public release of the Open Ethics Standard. The framework draws from documented patterns of corporate malfeasance, established regulatory frameworks (GDPR, FTC Click-to-Cancel, COPPA), certification standards (B Corp), and dark pattern taxonomies developed by UX researchers.

The standard is released under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0).
